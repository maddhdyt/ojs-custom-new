Florona : Jurnal Ilmiah Kesehatan (E-ISSN: 2829-3983, P-ISSN: 2829-3835), published by Arka Institute. These journals are openly accessible and subjected to peer review, and published twice a year (February and August). These journals will cover any and all fields of health-related basic and clinical research. A variety of general articles, reviews, and other high-quality research in the subject of health are also published in the journal. In addition to connecting and integrating the intellectual, methodological, and substantive diversity of study within the field of medicine, the journal's objective is to foster a healthy dialogue between medical researchers and medical practitioners whose work is being published. Articles that will encourage medical educators and researchers from all over the world to communicate with one another and share their thoughts are specifically sought by the publication.

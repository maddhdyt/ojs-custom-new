  • Histeria Jurnal: Ilmiah Soshum dan Humaniora


    Histeria: Jurnal Ilmiah Sosial dan Humaniora (E-ISSN : 2829-971X, P-ISSN : 2830-1447) is an open-access, peer-reviewed journal that is published twice a year, namely in January and July, and is bilingual (Indonesian and English) and published by ARKA INSTITUTE. Histeria: Jurnal Ilmiah Sosial dan Humaniora discusses understanding and analysing aspects of humans, society, and culture. As well as discussing contributions to a deeper understanding of humans, society and the world around us by developing insight, supporting policies and stimulating critical thinking on social, cultural and political issues that affect our lives.

    The journal encourages scholars and specialists from all social and humanities areas. Articles must be original, research-based, unpublished, and not currently under consideration for publication in other journals. All papers submitted are reviewed by the editors, editorial board, and blind reviewers.

  • Cessie : Jurnal Ilmiah Hukum

    Cessie : Jurnal Ilmiah Hukum(E-ISSN: 2830-0106, P-ISSN: 2830-0092) is a journal that publishes Focus & Scope research articles, which include:

    1. Criminal law
    2. Civil law
    3. International law
    4. Constitutional law
    5. Administrative Law
    6. Islamic law
    7. Economic Law
    8. Medical Law
    9. Customary law
    10. Environmental Law and other sections related to contemporary issues in law.

    This journal is published by the Arka Institute, which is published twice a year.

    All manuscripts submitted to the editor will receive a review using a double-blind review system, meaning that the author does not know the name of the reviewer and the reviewer does not know the name of the author. Cessie : Jurnal Ilmiah Hukum Journal has been registered with a Crossreff member number and has a unique DOI

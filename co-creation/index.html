nautical: Jurnal Ilmiah Teknik (E-ISSN: 2828-8548, P-ISSN: 2828-8696) is published twice a year, in February and August. Articles can be in the form of research results, scientific thoughts, or case studies in the fields of: civil engineering, which does not contain political elements; architecture; marine engineering; mining engineering; electrical engineering; industrial engineering; mechanical engineering; aeronautical engineering; informatics engineering; biomedical engineering; nuclear chemistry; shipping; and petroleum. This scientific journal contains articles on the results of thoughts and research results in the field of engineering that have never been published in scientific journals or other media. nautical: Jurnal Ilmiah Teknik is published by Arka Institute in collaboration with Asosiasi Peneliti ilmu Manajemen Indonesia.

Articles submitted to nautical: Jurnal Ilmiah Teknik will be reviewed with a blind peer-review system. The decision on the acceptance of a scientific article in this journal shall be the right of the Board of Editors based on recommendations from peer reviewers. Submit your manuscript today! via our online system. If you encounter any problems in submitting your manuscript, please contact us at jurnal@arkainstitute.co.id Download the nautical Author Guidelines for writing and language style here.
